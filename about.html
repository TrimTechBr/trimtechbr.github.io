About TrimTechBR

Two friends building practical technology solutions.

TrimTechBR is a collaborative initiative created by Henderson Andrade and Henrique Mizael to share knowledge and build practical open-source projects around cloud, infrastructure, automation and DevOps.

Our purpose

We believe that good technology projects should be useful, clear and well documented. TrimTechBR exists to transform field experience into reusable assets that help other professionals accelerate cloud and infrastructure initiatives.
