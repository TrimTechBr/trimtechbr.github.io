About TrimTechBR

Two friends building practical technology solutions.

TrimTechBR is a personal initiative created by two friends, Henderson Andrade and Henrique Mizael, to share knowledge and build practical open-source projects around cloud, infrastructure, automation and DevOps — in their spare time.

Our purpose

We believe that good technology projects should be useful, clear and well documented. TrimTechBR is how the two of us turn day-to-day field experience into open-source projects that help other professionals accelerate cloud and infrastructure initiatives.
